Need Help Right Now?

If you’re coming out of jail, on the street, or just trying not to slide back, you are not alone.

What we may be able to help with:
  • Hot meals (as resources allow)
  • Clothing and basic supplies
  • Prayer, conversation, and encouragement
  • Pointing you toward shelters and local services
How to reach us:

Email: BrokenGrowthMinistry7@proton.me

Subject line: “I Need Help” – tell us where you are and what you’re facing.

If you are in immediate danger or crisis, call local emergency services. Back to Home
