Our Mission

Broken Growth Ministries exists to walk beside people who are stepping out of incarceration, addiction, and homelessness. We know what it means to start from zero. Our mission is to make sure they don’t have to do it alone.

Faith in action.

We believe real faith is visible. A hot meal, a clean hoodie, a ride to a job interview, a prayer in a parking lot – these are the moments where hope cracks open a new life.

Growth, not perfection.

We don’t expect people to be perfect. We care that they are growing. Your support fuels small steps that add up to huge change over time.
