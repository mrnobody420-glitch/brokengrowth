What we actually do on the ground

This isn’t a big organization. There are no giant events or polished campaigns. It’s simple, steady work with people who are trying to get their feet under them again after homelessness, addiction, or time behind bars.

Basic survival needs

"I’m hungry, I’m cold, I’m tired."

Hot food, water, clean shirts, socks, hygiene items. When your basic needs are met, it gets a lot easier to make the next right decision.

A bit of my story

FROM BROKEN TO GROWING

"I didn’t start this as a project. I started it because I remember exactly what it feels like to stand on a sidewalk with nowhere to go, carrying a plastic bag with everything you own."

My name is Tim. I’ve been the guy people walk past. I’ve done time. I’ve slept outside. I’ve tried to rebuild my life with almost nothing. The only reason I’m still here is because a few people chose to see me as a human being and not a problem.

Broken Growth Ministries was born out of that. I don’t promise miracles. I promise to show up, tell the truth, and put whatever support comes in directly into the lives of people who are where I used to be.

WHY THIS MINISTRY EXISTS
