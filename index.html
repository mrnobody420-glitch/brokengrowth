Founded by Tim - breaking the cycle of homelessness and despair in Pensacola, one life at a time.

Our Mission

In Pensacola, too many good people are trapped in a cycle of homelessness, addiction, and broken systems. Broken Growth Ministries exists to give them what they deserve - compassion, dignity, and a path home. This ministry was born from lived experience. Tim, once homeless himself, now works to break that same cycle for others.

How You Can Help

Every dollar you give feeds, clothes, and shelters someone who needs a second chance. It’s not just a donation - it’s hope. Your support helps fund direct outreach, supplies, and safe transition housing. Together, we can make sure that no one is forgotten.
